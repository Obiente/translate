About Obiente Translate

Obiente Translate turns live conversations into a unified, readable stream with original + translated text. It’s optimized for events, meetings, and collaboration, with a Spotify-style lyrics view that’s easy to follow.

  • Multi-speaker interleaved feed with speaker labels and color-coded avatars
  • Sentence-level typing animation; current line centered and auto-scrolling
  • Fullscreen kiosk mode with participants bar and inactivity auto-fullscreen
  • Original text overlay and per-sentence metadata
  • Microphone and system audio inputs; robust multi-source handling
