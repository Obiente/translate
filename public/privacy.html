Privacy Policy

Obiente Translate processes audio and text live in memory to provide real-time transcription and translation. We do not store your conversation content on disk as part of this application—no data at rest by default.

  • Audio streams are processed live to provide transcription and translation.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Operational logs may be retained to improve reliability (never including raw audio or message content).

See our comprehensive Data Policy for organization-wide practices, including infrastructure and optional enterprise data retention settings.

For questions, contact support via your Obiente Cloud account portal.
